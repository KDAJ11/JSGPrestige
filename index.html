It's the moment a curious browser becomes an engaged prospect — because before they've even spoken to you, they've seen what's possible.

But how you implement it matters enormously. There are three viable paths, each with different build complexity, ongoing costs, and brand implications. Rather than force a decision, we've laid out all three honestly — with real time, real costs, and real trade-offs — so you can choose the one that matches where JSG Prestige is today and where it's going.

Our strong recommendation is Path One: The Signature Concierge Flow.
